About Us

E-Commerce Association - Leading Digital Innovation

The E-Commerce Association is a nonprofit professional organization dedicated to driving innovation and fostering digital transformation in Saudi Arabia.

Our mission is to be the leading force in e-commerce development, providing awareness, training, and industry partnerships to empower businesses and professionals.

Mission

To lead the transformation of e-commerce in Saudi Arabia by providing education, training, and strategic partnerships that enhance business capabilities.

We strive to empower entrepreneurs, businesses, and the community with digital commerce knowledge and opportunities.

Vision

To become the leading digital business hub in Saudi Arabia, fostering innovation, sustainability, and a thriving e-commerce ecosystem.

Our vision aligns with Saudi Vision 2030, aiming to support entrepreneurs, create digital jobs, and empower the next generation of tech leaders.
